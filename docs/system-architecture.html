2. Indicators & Market Data Used

Volume Indicator
Open Interest (OI)
OI = Total outstanding contracts (not yet settled)
High Put OI at a strike → Acts as support (writers won't let it breach)
High Call OI at a strike → Acts as resistance
OI Buildup + Price rise → Long buildup (bullish)
OI Buildup + Price fall → Short buildup (bearish)
Scoring: OI > 1M → 25pts, > 500K → 20pts, > 100K → 15pts
Volatility Indicator
Implied Volatility (IV)
IV is extracted from market option prices via Black-Scholes model (Exchange provides IV in MODE_FULL ticks)
High IV (>75th percentile) → Options are expensive → Good for selling
Low IV (<25th percentile) → Options are cheap → Good for buying
IV Crush → If IV drops >20% from entry → Consider exit
Scoring: IV 15-35% → 15pts (sweet spot for buying)
Liquidity Indicator
Volume & Bid-Ask Spread
Spread % = ((Ask - Bid) / LTP) × 100 Volume = Number of contracts traded today
Volume > 100K → Highly liquid → 20pts
Spread < 0.5% → Very tight → 15pts (low slippage)
Spread > 2% → SKIP (too illiquid, entry/exit will be costly)
Min premium: Skip options below ₹50 (wide spreads)
Price Indicator
Last Traded Price (LTP) & Spot
ATM Strike = round(Spot / StrikeStep) × StrikeStep NIFTY StrikeStep = 50, BANKNIFTY StrikeStep = 100
ATM ± 10 strikes scanned on each cycle
Preferred premium range: ₹100-500 → 10pts
Delta 0.3-0.5 → Slightly OTM to ATM (best risk/reward)

3. Black-Scholes Greeks — Calculations

Δ
Delta
Price sensitivity to ₹1 move in spot
CE: N(d₁) PE: N(d₁) - 1
Γ
Gamma
Rate of change of Delta
N'(d₁) / (S·σ·√T)
Θ
Theta
Daily time decay (₹/day)
-(S·N'(d₁)·σ)/(2√T) ± rKe⁻ʳᵀN(±d₂) / 365
ν
Vega
Sensitivity to 1% IV change
S · N'(d₁) · √T / 100

Core Black-Scholes Formulas

d₁ = [ln(S/K) + (r + σ²/2)·T] / (σ·√T) d₂ = d₁ - σ·√T Where: S = Spot price (e.g., NIFTY at 22,500) K = Strike price (e.g., 22,500) T = Time to expiry in years (e.g., 5 days = 5/365 = 0.0137) r = Risk-free rate (6.5% = 0.065) σ = Implied Volatility (decimal, e.g., 0.15 for 15%) N() = Cumulative normal distribution N'() = Normal probability density function

Position Sizing Formula

risk_per_unit = |Entry - Stop Loss| max_units = MAX_LOSS_PER_TRADE / risk_per_unit = ₹2,000 / risk_per_unit lots = floor(max_units / lot_size) lots = max(lots, 1) ← At least 1 lot quantity = lots × lot_size Example: Entry = ₹185, SL = ₹150, Lot = 75 Risk/unit = ₹35 Max units = 2000/35 = 57.1 Lots = floor(57.1/75) = 0 → 1 (minimum) Quantity = 75

7. Exit Logic — Trailing SL & Auto-Exit

1. Stop Loss Hit
Price ≤ SL → Immediate MARKET exit. No delay, no second-guessing.
2. Target Hit
Price ≥ Target → Immediate MARKET exit. Book full profit.
3. Trailing SL — Breakeven
When price reaches 50% of target move → Trail SL to entry (breakeven). Risk-free trade.
4. Trailing SL — Profit Lock
When price reaches 75% of target move → Trail SL to lock 50% of unrealized profit.
5. Time-Based Exit
3:15 PM for regular days, 3:20 PM on expiry days. Auto square-off all MIS.
6. Max Holding Time
If held > 2 hours with < 10% profit → Exit. Don't let theta eat your premium.

Trailing SL Calculation

total_move = Target - Entry current_move = Current Price - Entry move_percent = (current_move / total_move) × 100 IF move_percent ≥ 50%: new_SL = Entry (breakeven) IF move_percent ≥ 75%: new_SL = Entry + current_move × 0.50 (lock half the profit) Example: Entry=₹185, Target=₹250, SL=₹150 total_move = ₹65 Price hits ₹217.50 (50% of ₹65 = ₹32.50): → SL trails from ₹150 to ₹185 (breakeven) Price hits ₹233.75 (75% of ₹65 = ₹48.75): → SL trails to ₹185 + ₹48.75×0.5 = ₹209.38 (locked ₹24.38 profit)

8. Charges Calculation

Brokerage = ₹20 flat per executed order (buy + sell = ₹40) STT = 0.0625% on sell-side turnover (options) GST = 18% on brokerage Stamp Duty = 0.003% on buy-side turnover SEBI Charges = 0.0001% on total turnover Exchange Txn = 0.00053% on total turnover (NSE) Total Turnover = (Entry + Exit) × Quantity Example (NIFTY CE, 1 lot = 75 units): Buy ₹185 × 75 = ₹13,875 Sell ₹238 × 75 = ₹17,850 Turnover = ₹31,725 Brokerage = ₹40.00 STT = ₹17,850 × 0.000625 = ₹11.16 GST = ₹40 × 0.18 = ₹7.20 Stamp = ₹13,875 × 0.00003 = ₹0.42 SEBI = ₹31,725 × 0.000001 = ₹0.03 Exchange = ₹31,725 × 0.0000053 = ₹0.17 ───────────────────────────────── Total Charges ≈ ₹58.98 Gross P&L = (238-185) × 75 = ₹3,975 Net P&L = ₹3,975 - ₹58.98 = ₹3,916.02
